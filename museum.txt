******************************* WELCOME TO THE GALLERIA *******************************
Galleria is the exlcusive Art Gallery in the Meta Region. The permanent collection comprises more than 79 000 works representing nearly 2000 years of 
extraordinary European, Canadian, modern and contemporary art - paintings by such masters as Rembrandt, Hals, Poussin, Chardin, Delacroix, Renoir and Picasso, 
and sculptures by Rodin, Degas and Matisse. The gallery's Henry Moore Sculpture Centre holds the world's largest and most comprehensive public collection 
of Moore works anywhere.
